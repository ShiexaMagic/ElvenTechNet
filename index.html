World only Flexible, Inexpensive, Lightweight and Fireproof Composite Material

Elven FireGuard™ is our next-generation fireproof composite, developed to meet the toughest safety and performance demands. Lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective, it withstands extreme temperatures up to 2000°C (3600°F) while maintaining exceptional 3D formability. Available in several tailored versions, FireGuard™ is redefining fire protection across construction, aerospace, EVs, and other high-risk industries.

About Elven Technologies

Founded in October 2022, Elven Technologies began with a vision to create revolutionary fireproof solutions. Our name draws inspiration from elves and fantasy—where "advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic."

What started as a small team working in a 15m² garage has evolved into a groundbreaking company developing solutions that withstand temperatures up to 2000°C. Our flagship technology provides critical protection for lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles and transportation chains, preventing catastrophic fires from becoming disasters.

After extensive laboratory testing with Underwriters Laboratories (UL) in the United States and numerous real-world trials, our composite solutions have consistently outperformed conventional alternatives. Even our most basic models demonstrate exceptional fire resistance that only solutions like concrete can match.
