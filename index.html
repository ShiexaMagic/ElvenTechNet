About Elven Technologies

Founded in 2019, Elven Technologies began with a vision to create revolutionary fireproof materials. Our name draws inspiration from elves and fantasy—where "advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic."

What started as a small team working in a 15m² garage has evolved into a groundbreaking company developing materials that withstand temperatures up to 2000°C. Our flagship technology provides critical protection for lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles and transportation chains, preventing catastrophic fires from becoming disasters.

After extensive laboratory testing with Underwriters Laboratories (UL) in the United States and numerous real-world trials, our composite materials have consistently outperformed conventional solutions. Even our most basic models demonstrate exceptional fire resistance that only materials like concrete can match.

EV Battery Fire Containment System

Vamekh, CTO of Elven Technologies, demonstrates the world's first full containment of an electric vehicle (EV) lithium battery fire. Using our innovative fireproof composite material, we built a protective enclosure for a 400V, 17.6kWh lithium battery pack and put it through a series of rigorous explosion tests.

In this demonstration:

  • The construction and setup of the battery enclosure
  • Three different test scenarios: unprotected, protected without exhaust, and protected with exhaust
  • Real-time temperature readings and comparisons

This breakthrough technology has applications in electric vehicles, battery storage facilities, and transportation of hazardous materials.
